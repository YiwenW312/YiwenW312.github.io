About Me

Hello! I'm Emelina (Yiwen) Wang, a software engineer with a unique blend of legal expertise and technical skills. I founded Tech@Crossroads, a student organization that encourages interdisciplinary collaboration, merging my legal and technical backgrounds. Our projects bridge the gap between tech and non-tech students, fostering innovation and shared learning.

As a problem solver, quick learner, and team player, I am eager to grow and make an impact in a Software Engineer position. My diverse skills and experiences make me a versatile and valuable asset in the software engineering field.
